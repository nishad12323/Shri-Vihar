About me


Hello! I'm Pavithra Aravind, the founder of Shri Vihar, a spiritual initiative dedicated to imparting devotional Slokas, Bhajans, and essential life practices through captivating stories from revered epics such as the Ramayana and Mahabharata. What began in 2019 as a modest endeavor with a few local children has blossomed into a cherished community tradition, fueled by the enthusiastic participation of both kids and their families.

My passion lies in nurturing young minds and helping them discover and develop their talents. The children from Shri Vihar have had the opportunity to showcase their skills by reciting various Slokas, including Ashtakams and Pancharatnams, at events like Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations at Sri Ganesha Temple in Dallas, TX, as well as other special occasions.

I seek the blessings of Lord Venkateshwara to grant me the wisdom and strength needed to continue this meaningful journey.




Classes


Sloka classes are offered online as well as in-person. The class syllabus is given below:

September to October — Each week, 4 lines of slokas will be taught from simple ashtakams. Examples: Krishna ashtakam and Linga ashtakam

November to January — Each week, 4-6 lines of sloka will be taught. Examples: Stotras like Ashtalakshmi Stotram and Dhukka nivarana Ashtakam

February to May — Each week, 6-8 lines of Sloka will be taught. Examples: Adithya Hrudayam and Sri Mahalakshmi Stotram
