Policies



  • ShriVihar Slokam classes start in September and end by the last week of April.
  • ShriVihar Summer Slokam Classes start in June and end by the last week of July.
  • Classes will be on break during long weekend holidays.
  • The classes are conducted free of charge. (Student's interest and active participation is expected as gratitude, please no gift cards or any other gifts).
  • Please have your kids join the class on time.
  • The kids need to maintain a separate Slokam folder, and the pdf will be shared in the respective WhatsApp group.
  • Slokam recordings will be posted in the respective WhatsApp group.The kids need to send their sloka recording before the next class.
  • Please inform me in cases of absences or delays to join the class.
